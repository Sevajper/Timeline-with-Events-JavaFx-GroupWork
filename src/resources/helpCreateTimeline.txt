1. Open Timeline-manager.
2. Click "Add Timeline" button.
3. Provide a name for the Timeline in the "Timeline Name " section.
4. Provide Start and End time of the timeline. Start and end time has 
	to be written with years-month-days ("000-00-00"). 
	Alternatively press the smaller calendar button and choose the date 
	with the Datepicker function. The month are picked in the upper left corner 
	in the Datepicker window and the years are chosen in the upper right corner. 
	Alternate between the years with left and right arrows.
5. When done, click the "Finish"button. 
6. A new Timeline will appear
